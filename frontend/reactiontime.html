Reaction Time

On the screen, you'll see a red circle and a blue circle. Your job is to
press the matching button (or tap the circle) as fast as you can when you
see the color change. So, if the red circle flashes, press the red button!
If the blue circle flashes, press the blue button! Try your best to be speedy,
but don't worry if you make a mistake. It's all about seeing how fast you can
react!
