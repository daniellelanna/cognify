Memory Match

You'll see colored boxes appear on the screen, each with a word inside. Your
job is to check three things:
  1. The color of the box
  2. The color of the word
  3. The word itself
If all things match perfectly - like a red box with the word 'red' written in red
letters - click the box as quickly as you can. But, even if one thing is
different, don't click! It's like a speedy puzzle, so try your best to be
quick and accurate!
